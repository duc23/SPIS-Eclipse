Niagara Falls, Ontario

Would you like to experience the wonders of the iconic water falls that straddle between the international border of Canada and the United States? Collectively known as the Niagara Falls, the Horseshoe Falls, the American Falls, and the Bridal Veil is notable for its beauty and valuable source of hydroelectric power. Therefore, if you would like to experience the eclispe and the falls simultaneously then come to Niagara Falls!


Gander, Newfoundland Island

Gander is a small town that have a population of more than 11,000. Though it does not have prominent features like other provinces in Canada, it does have pleasant places to watch the elcipse such as the Cobbs Pond Rotary Park and Gander Heritage Memorial Park.
