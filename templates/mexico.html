Welcome to Mexico!

Below is a list of the best locations that offer the best scenery for the eclipse!

Mazatlán, Sinaloa


Along with its delicate food and lovely weather, the resort town, Mazatlán, provides an irresistible 12 miles of sandy beaches and a beautiful skyline for many to enjoy. So remember to join pack some sunscreen and eclipse glasses and enjoy the view!


Durango, Durango


Durango is well known for its colonial history. Its architecture resembles many European elements ranging from Barcelona, Florence, Paris, and Madrid. One of its widely known building called the Cathedral Basilica of the Immaculate Conception is recognized as the most beautiful and most significant architectural representation of the state. Therefore, if you would like to experience the history of Mexico and also see the elclipse, then feel free to come to Durango!


Torreón, Coahuila


Would you like to see third tallest statue of Christ in Latin America? Would you like to experience the eclipse and have the view of the entire city together at once? If you answered "yes" to any of the question, then come down to Torreón!
