Below is a list of the best locations that offer the best scenery for the eclipse!

Buffalo, New York

Would you like to watch how the glorious Niagara Falls changes when it turn from day to night and back to day again? Why not book a boat tour to have this amazing experience? There are many other sites to see like visiting the different architectural structures, such as the HH Richardson complex and the Darwin D. Martin House, and tons of green space for many people experience the eclispe.


Rochester, New York

Rochester is a city located near Lake Ontario, which is known as one of the five Great Lakes of North America. The city is recognized as "the Flower City" with its scenic parks and its annual Lilac Festival where hundreds of different lilacs bloom, and thousands of visitors arrive. So why not catch a eclipse out on the water or watch the eclipse while being surrounded by nature's beauty?


Dallas, Texas

There are many things to do in Dallas during the spring time, such as visiting the Sixth Floor Museum, the Dallas Arboretum and Botanical Garden, and the Stockyards in Fort Worth. But the best way to view the eclispe is to get on high grounds, so try experiencing it from the Reunion Tower!
